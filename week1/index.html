Game Overview

Welcome to the badminton tournament registration page. Badminton is a racquet sport played using racquets to hit a shuttlecock across a net.

It is a sport that requires agility, strength, and precision. The game can be played by two opposing players (singles) or two opposing pairs (doubles).

Game Highlights

  • Olympic Sport since 1992
  • Top players include Lin Dan, Lee Chong Wei, and Saina Nehwal
  • Annual All England Open Badminton Championships
